Sundar Pichai

Indian-American Business Executive

Full Name: Sundararajan Pichai

Date Of Birth: July 12, 1972

Place Of Birth: Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India

Height: 5 ft 11 inches

Education: IIT Kharagpur, Stanford University, University of Pennsylvania

Occupation: CEO of Alphabet Inc. and Google

Major Achievements: Led development of Chrome browser, Android OS growth, CEO of major tech firms

Father: Regunatha Pichai

Mother: Lakshmi Pichai

Specialization: Technology and Leadership

Sundar Pichai is known for his leadership at Google and its parent company Alphabet. Born in Madurai, he rose to prominence through innovation and effective management. After completing his engineering at IIT Kharagpur, he pursued higher studies in the United States. Joining Google in 2004, he played a key role in developing the Chrome browser and later assumed leadership roles, becoming the CEO of Google in 2015 and Alphabet in 2019.
